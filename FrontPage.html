Explore the Tourist Places in Ganjam District, Odisha

Religious Sites

  • Budhi Thakurani Temple

    A temple in Bada Bazar that's been around for over 2 centuries.

  • Mahuri Kalua Temple

    A temple on top of the Kerandi mountains that's believed to have helped kings fight the British.

  • Markandi Beach

    A beach with a temple that blends Telugu and Odia architecture.

Beaches

  • Gopalpur Beach

    A beautiful coastal destination known for its scenic beauty and tranquility.

Hill Ranges

  • Taptapani

    A hill range with hot water springs and picturesque landscapes.

Other Attractions

  • Nirmaljhar

    Also known as The Pure Stream, this is a religious site.

  • Humuri Tampara

    A sweet water lake that offers a serene and peaceful environment.

  • Breakfast Island

    A clean island with a small canopy for picnics, perfect for a day trip.

  • Olive Ridley Turtles at Rushikulya

    A conservation site where you can witness the nesting of Olive Ridley turtles.
